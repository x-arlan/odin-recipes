Homemade ice-cream

Description

Cook homemade healthy ice cream and make happy your relatives and friends!

A recipe for a light dessert that will surprise and conquer everyone. Especially such a creamy, tasty, as in childhood. There are no indifferent people, everyone will ask for more!

Ingredients

  • 160 ml of milk;
  • 3 egg yolks;
  • 150 grams of sugar
  • 500 g cream for whipping;
  • a little vanilla extract.

Steps

  1. Heat the milk over moderate heat, but do not boil. Separately, whisk together the yolks and sugar. Sugar should be completely dissolved.
  2. Pour the mixture into a saucepan and mix it constantly with a spatula, bring to a slight thickening over medium heat.
  3. Place the saucepan in a container of ice water and, stirring constantly, cool to room temperature. Whip the cold cream with a mixer until creamy.
  4. Pour the milk-egg mixture into the cream through a sieve. Add vanilla extract and stir until smooth. Put the mass in a wide form, cover with a film and put in the freezer for 3-4 hours.
Enjoy your chilly masterpiece!
