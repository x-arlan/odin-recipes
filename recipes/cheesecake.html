New-York Cheesecake

Description

If you are looking for a good cheesecake recipe, look no further. Cheesecake "New York" with a delicate silky texture and light notes of lemon.

Cheesecake is very easy to cook, however the process itself takes a lot of time, but the result is worth it!

Ingredients

For the base

  • Shortbread cookies or unsalted crackers - 350 g
  • Butter - 200 g + for greasing the mold
  • Sugar - 2 tbsp. l.

For filling

  • Cream cheese (Philadelphia) - 1 kg
  • sugar 250 grams
  • eggs 5 pcs.
  • Corn starch - 3 tbsp.
  • Lemon juice - 2 tbsp.
  • Lemon zest - 2 tsp
  • Vanilla extract - 2 tsp
  • Sour cream 20% - 100 g

For cover

  • Sour cream 20% - 150 g
  • Powdered sugar - 2 tbsp.
  • Lemon juice - 2 tsp

For decoration

  • Fruits
  • Berries

Steps

  1. In a blender, grind the cookies into small crumbs. Pour the melted butter into the cookie crumbs, add sugar and mix well. We cover the bottom of the form with baking paper, grease the edges with butter. We spread the crumbs in the form, then we tamp the bottom.

    Bake the cheesecake base at 175°C for 10 minutes, then cool completely.

  2. Stir in the cream cheese until it is soft and smooth. Add sugar in small portions and continue to mix. Add cornstarch, lemon juice, lemon zest, vanilla essence and mix for literally 15-20 seconds. Then add sour cream and mix again for 15-20 seconds. Add one egg at a time, mixing well after each added. Pour the cheese filling over the biscuit base and smooth the surface well.

    Bake the cheesecake for the first 10 minutes at a temperature of 200 ° C, then reduce the temperature to 120 ° C and bake for another 60-90 minutes. After baking, turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake with the door closed for 30 minutes.Then open the oven door and leave for another 30 minutes.

    *baking time may vary depending on your oven

  3. While the cheesecake is baking, prepare the sour cream layer. Mix sour cream with powdered sugar and lemon juice.
  4. We take the cheesecake out of the oven and, while it is still hot, grease the entire surface with sour cream. Then leave the cheesecake to cool at room temperature.
  5. After cooling completely, send the cheesecake to the refrigerator for at least 6-8 hours.
  6. Carefully take away the form in which the cheesecake was baked. Better to put on a big plate. And, finally, decorate the cheesecake with fresh fruits and berries.
